About

My name is Irma (pronounced /ˈiː.mə/ or eema). Born in Phnom Penh, Cambodia in 2000, based in France since 2013.

I like to create things and see them come to life. Learning new technology to make things even prettier and flashier is what drives me. When I'm not coding, you'll find me traveling or creating something just for the joy of it.

🎮 How It All Started

It started with editing Tumblr and DeviantArt themes. Then video games sparked an interest in game development, which evolved into building web and mobile applications.

I wasn't the best at drawing, but I could still create the worlds in my head through code — and that felt amazing. From the art community to the dev community.
